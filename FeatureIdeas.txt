Quest Ideas

  1. Broken Stone Ring
      - The dark priest’s ritual damages a stone ring.
      - Collect Yarrow, Mugwort, and a rare rune fragment to restore or corrupt it.
      - Choice changes teleport destinations or encounter difficulty.

  2. The Missing Scroll
      - The scroll priest requests a unique scroll stolen by Wraiths.
      - A stronger named Wraith appears at night.
      - Reward: discounted scrolls or an exclusive spell.

  3. Apple Blight
      - Trees near Tambry stop producing apples.
      - Gather Mandrake and Yarrow, then cast Harvest near affected trees.
      - Reward: ranger discounts and occasional rare herbs near trees.

  4. The Silent Skeleton
      - One prayer skeleton stops chanting.
      - Find its missing bone and return it to the stone circle.
      - Reward: a clue about the dark priest or a hidden stash.

  5. Merchant Rivalry
      - Herb wizard and scroll priest each request a rare ingredient.
      - Help one, both, or neither.
      - Rewards affect shop prices and available stock.

  6. Night Procession
      - At night, Wraiths travel toward the dark priest’s ring.
      - Defeat groups before they arrive.
      - Reward scales with the number intercepted.

  Feature Ideas

  1. Named Elite Enemies
      - Rare variants with higher vitality and guaranteed loot.
      - Examples: Wraith Seer, Skeleton Captain, Venomous Serpent.

  2. Shop Stock Limits
      - Merchants restock after sleeping or camping.
      - Prevents unlimited access to powerful ingredients and scrolls.

  3. Rare Ingredients
      - Add Mooncap, Grave Dust, Emberleaf, and Silverthorn.
      - Reserve them for quests and advanced spells.

  4. Advanced Spells
      - Blink: Mugwort + Yarrow, short teleport.
      - Thorns: Mandrake + Bloodroot, damages attackers.
      - Dispel: Yarrow + Nightshade, removes nearby magical hazards.
      - Summon Light: Yarrow + Mugwort, longer Light effect.

  5. Quest Journal
      - Add List > Quests.
      - Show active objectives and completed quests in the message area.

  6. World Events
      - Night-only encounters, merchant caravans, shrine rituals, and temporary herb patches.

  7. Stone Ring Destinations
      - Require discovery before destinations appear.
      - Some rings could activate only at night or while carrying a specific ingredient.

  8. Camp Events
      - Chance of ambush, dream clues, healing bonuses, or herb discoveries when camping.
